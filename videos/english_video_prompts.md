# Ageless Strength - English Video Narration Scripts

## Video 01: Chair Squat for Beginners
**Filename:** 01_chair_squat.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Chair Squat for Beginners. This exercise helps strengthen your legs and improve balance. 

Step 1: Stand in front of a sturdy chair with feet shoulder-width apart.
Step 2: Slowly lower your body until you gently touch the chair seat.
Step 3: Stand back up using your leg muscles.
Step 4: Keep your back straight throughout the movement.

Repeat this exercise 10 times. Remember to breathe steadily and move slowly."

## Video 02: Wall Push-Up
**Filename:** 02_wall_pushup.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Wall Push-Up exercise. This is a safe way to strengthen your arms and chest.

Step 1: Stand facing a wall, about arm's length away.
Step 2: Place your hands flat on the wall at shoulder height.
Step 3: Slowly bend your elbows and lean toward the wall.
Step 4: Push back to the starting position.

Repeat 12 times. Keep your body straight like a plank."

## Video 03: Resistance Band Arm Training
**Filename:** 03_resistance_band_bicep.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Resistance Band Arm Training. This exercise builds arm strength.

Step 1: Stand on the middle of the resistance band with both feet.
Step 2: Hold the band handles with your palms facing up.
Step 3: Curl your hands up toward your shoulders, squeezing your biceps.
Step 4: Slowly lower your hands back down.

Repeat 15 times for each arm. Use a light resistance band to start."

## Video 04: Resistance Band Leg Curl
**Filename:** 04_resistance_band_legcurl.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Resistance Band Leg Curl. This exercise strengthens your back leg muscles.

Step 1: Anchor the resistance band to a sturdy piece of furniture.
Step 2: Loop the band around your right ankle.
Step 3: Bend your knee and pull your heel toward your bottom.
Step 4: Slowly straighten your leg back out.

Repeat 12 times on each leg. Hold onto a chair for balance."

## Video 05: Seated Leg Raise
**Filename:** 05_seated_leg_raise.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Seated Leg Raise. This exercise strengthens your thigh muscles.

Step 1: Sit on a sturdy chair with your feet flat on the floor.
Step 2: Extend your right leg out straight in front of you.
Step 3: Hold for 3 seconds, keeping your thigh tight.
Step 4: Slowly lower your foot back down.

Repeat 10 times on each leg. Keep your back straight against the chair."

## Video 06: Dumbbell Shoulder Press
**Filename:** 06_dumbbell_shoulder.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Dumbbell Shoulder Press. This exercise builds shoulder strength.

Step 1: Sit on a chair with a back support, holding light dumbbells.
Step 2: Start with dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing forward.
Step 3: Press the dumbbells straight up overhead.
Step 4: Slowly lower them back to shoulder height.

Repeat 10 times. Start with very light weights, like 1 to 2 pounds."

## Video 07: Bodyweight Squat Basics
**Filename:** 07_bodyweight_squat.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Bodyweight Squat Basics. This exercise strengthens your entire lower body.

Step 1: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, facing a chair for support.
Step 2: Slowly bend your knees as if sitting down.
Step 3: Lower yourself halfway, keeping your knees over your toes.
Step 4: Push through your heels to stand back up.

Repeat 8 times. Use the chair for balance if needed. Move slowly and controlled."

## Video 08: Advanced Wall Push-Up
**Filename:** 08_wall_pushup_advanced.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Advanced Wall Push-Up. This is a more challenging version of the wall push-up.

Step 1: Stand facing the wall, lean forward and place hands on the wall.
Step 2: Walk your feet back a few steps to increase the angle.
Step 3: Bend your elbows and lower your chest toward the wall.
Step 4: Push back to the starting position with control.

Repeat 15 times. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels."

## Video 09: Resistance Band Full Body Combo

## Video 10: Balance Training
**Filename:** 10_balance_training.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Balance Training. This exercise helps prevent falls by improving your balance.

Step 1: Stand behind a sturdy chair, holding it for support.
Step 2: Lift your left foot off the ground.
Step 3: Hold this single-leg stance for 10 seconds.
Step 4: Lower your foot and repeat with the other leg.

Repeat 5 times on each leg. Focus on a fixed point ahead to help with balance."

## Video 12: Glute Bridge
**Filename:** 13_glute_bridge.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Glute Bridge. This exercise strengthens your hip muscles.

Step 1: Lie on your back on a comfortable surface with knees bent.
Step 2: Keep your feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart.
Step 3: Lift your hips toward the ceiling, squeezing your glutes.
Step 4: Hold for 3 seconds, then slowly lower your hips back down.

Repeat 12 times. Keep your core engaged and breathe normally."

## Video 15: Plank Basics
**Filename:** 16_plank_basic.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Plank Basics. This exercise strengthens your core muscles.

Step 1: Start on your hands and knees on a comfortable surface.
Step 2: Lower down onto your forearms, keeping elbows under shoulders.
Step 3: Extend your legs behind you, balancing on your toes.
Step 4: Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels. Hold for 10 seconds.

Repeat 5 times with short rests in between. Don't let your hips sag down."

## Video 16: Lunges Training
**Filename:** 17_lunges.mp4
**Narration:**
"Welcome to Lunges Training. This exercise strengthens your legs and improves balance.

Step 1: Stand behind a sturdy chair, holding it for support.
Step 2: Step forward with your right leg, lowering your hips.

## Video 18: Core Stability Training
